PMV Portugal

Fashion sourcing & facilitation

Facilitation for the fashion industry

PMV is a company based in Northern Portugal acting as a facilitator between fashion brands and manufacturing plants. We connect production units and businesses in Portugal, Spain and Morocco, ensuring competitive solutions in terms of price, quality and production capacity.

Our focus is straightforward: finding the right factory for each product, with the level of service that international markets expect.

What we do

  • Sourcing & factory selection in Portugal, Spain and Morocco.
  • Operational liaison between brands, agents and manufacturing units.
  • Development support for collections, pre-costing, sampling and orders.
  • Focus on price and service, building long-term, trust-based partnerships.

PMV collaborates with several industrial players, including reference companies in the textile and apparel sector such as Twintex, as well as other units in Northern Portugal, Spain and Morocco.
